# Section 12: Worked examples, before and after

Each example shows a common mistake, the corrected version, and the reason. Use these as patterns, not as copy to reuse verbatim.

| Before | After | Why |
|--------|-------|-----|
| Envision serves the visually impaired across Kansas. | Envision serves people who are blind or have low vision across Kansas. | Person-first. The word _impaired_ is banned in every form. |
| As a Wichita-based nonprofit, we're proud to give back through quality print services. | Envision delivers print and fulfillment with documented on-time performance and the capacity enterprise programs plan around. | Commercial work leads with capability, not charity or giving back. No nonprofit opener. |
| Envision is headquartered in Wichita, Kansas. | Envision operates across Wichita and the Dallas area. | Never _headquartered in Wichita_. Describe market presence instead. |
| Despite her vision loss, Maria learned to live independently with our help. | With the right training and tools, Maria built the independent routine she wanted. | No despite framing. Center the person's agency, not Envision's rescue. |
| Our forward-thinking curriculum serves children of all abilities. | Our future-ready curriculum supports children who are blind or have low vision alongside sighted peers. | Future-ready, not forward-thinking. No _all abilities_. State the integration model accurately. |
| The White Cane Day Walk benefits the Envision Dallas Foundation. | The White Cane Day Walk supports Envision's Dallas Foundation. | The apostrophe is required. Never _Envision Dallas Foundation_. |
| We utilize cutting-edge research to optimize outcomes for the blind. | Envision's Research Institute studies [named study] to improve outcomes for people who are blind or have low vision. | Use, not utilize. Improve, not optimize. Name the research. Person-first. |
