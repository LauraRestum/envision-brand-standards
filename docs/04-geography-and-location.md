# Section 4: Geography and location

All location guidance lives here. Define the terms once, then apply them by context, audience, and level of specificity.

| Term | Use it for |
|------|------------|
| **Dallas / Dallas area** | Market recognition: public-facing language, donor messaging, recruiting headlines, community awareness. |
| **Farmers Branch** | Physical-facility accuracy for the Dallas-area site: addresses, directions, event logistics, interview and worksite details, site-specific references. |
| **North Texas** | Regional impact: service reach, community need, donor impact, broader regional growth. |
| **Wichita** | Market recognition for the Wichita market, and Wichita facility accuracy. |
| **Envision Wichita** | Internal reference to the Wichita, Water Street facility in internal communications only. |
| **Envision Dallas** | Internal reference to the Farmers Branch facility in internal communications only. |

## Primary rule

Do not describe Envision as headquartered in Wichita. Choose location references by context, audience, and level of specificity, and use **Wichita and the Dallas area** when describing Envision's primary market presence.

## Make local relevance visible

When creating ads, campaigns, recruiting, donor communications, or program marketing for a specific local market, make the local relevance clear in the message itself: in the headline, subhead, copy, call to action, proof point, statistic, story, event detail, or audience description. Do not rely on a local logo lockup, foundation name, or program name alone to signal local relevance.

**Pattern.** Envision + local market or audience + specific service, opportunity, or impact.

### Examples

- Envision is expanding access to early childhood education for families in the Dallas area.
- Envision helps people with vision loss across North Texas build skills, independence and connection.
- Envision is hiring in the Dallas area for roles that support meaningful employment and mission growth. Positions are based at Envision's Farmers Branch facility.
- Envision donors help expand opportunity for people who are blind or have low vision in North Texas.

## Avoid

- Headquartered in Wichita.
- Wichita-based nonprofit, unless the context is intentionally local.
- Dallas location when an address, facility, directions, or site logistics are involved. Use Farmers Branch.
- Farmers Branch in broad public-awareness copy when Dallas area or North Texas would be clearer.
- Envision Dallas as shorthand for the full organization.
- Broad enterprise language in local campaigns without making the local audience, need, or impact clear.
